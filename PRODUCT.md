# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, technical leads, clients, and collaborators who need to evaluate Wesley Correia's professional experience, delivery history, and engineering judgment quickly.

## Product Purpose

This is Wesley Correia's public professional portfolio. It presents skills, experience, certifications, personal repositories, professional cases, and an AI chat grounded in portfolio content. Success means visitors can distinguish professional work from personal projects and reach the right evidence with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Pragmatic, technical, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page tropes, decorative visuals that obscure project evidence, and over-styled portfolio effects that make the work harder to inspect.

## Design Principles

- Lead with verifiable work and clear navigation.
- Keep professional cases and personal repositories distinct.
- Preserve the existing responsive portfolio language instead of introducing a competing visual system.
- Prefer accessible, crawlable links for public content.
- Make interactive elements feel deliberate and useful, not decorative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Public pages should preserve keyboard access, visible focus, semantic headings, descriptive image alternatives, reduced-motion support, and sufficient text contrast.
